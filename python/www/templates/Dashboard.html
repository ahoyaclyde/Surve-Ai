Frequently Asked Questions

Quech your anxiety through laid out principles first hand

How does {{CompanyID}} improve video surveillance security ?

Blockchain enhances the security of video surveillance by decentralizing storage, which reduces the risk of data breaches. It ensures that video data cannot be altered or deleted without a trace, maintaining the integrity of the footage.

What role does AI play in this system ?

AI enhances surveillance by providing intelligent analytics such as facial recognition, object detection, and behavior analysis. These capabilities allow for real-time monitoring and actionable insights from the collected video data.

Who can access the video footage ?

Access to video footage can be controlled through smart contracts that define permissions. Only authorized individuals and entities can view or manipulate the data, ensuring that privacy is maintained.

Can footage be altered once it is recorded ?

No, once footage is recorded and linked to the blockchain, it cannot be altered or deleted. This provides a secure and trustworthy source of evidence for legal and security purposes.

How does this system comply with data protection regulations ?

Our blockchain-based surveillance system is designed with data protection regulations in mind, such as GDPR. Features like encryption, user consent management, and audit trails help ensure compliance.
